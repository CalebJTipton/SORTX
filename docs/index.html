A powerful yet intuitve data-oriented windows utility to efficiently sort multiple types of data by deeply customizable user-defined perameters.

With just a few clicks, you can categorize and sort your files into your own customizable organization scheme. Whether you have a large number junkyard files or just collections scattered across different locations, SORT X makes it easy to keep your files organized.
